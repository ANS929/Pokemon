Converting Units

Introduction:

We can convert between units in the same system.

Conversion:

Inches and feet are units of measurement. A foot is made up of 12 inches. We can easily convert between feet and inches. To convert a number in feet to inches, multiply it by 12.

Example: Rayquaza is 23 feet tall. That means Rayquaza is 23 * 12 = 276 inches tall.
