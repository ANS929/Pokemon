Multiplication of Whole Numbers

Introduction:

Multiplication is the process of adding a number to itself a particular number of times. The answer to a multiplication problem is called the product.

Multiplication:

To multiply two numbers, begin by lining them up as you do with addition or subtraction. Starting with the ones position of the bottom number, multiply it by each position of the top number, moving from right to left. Carry as needed. If the bottom number has a second digit (a tens position), begin by adding a 0 as a placeholder. Then, repeat the same process as you did with the first digit. Add both of the products to get your final answer.

Example 1: Your active Pokémon can do 220 damage. What is the total damage done by your Pokémon if it uses that attack 6 times?

220 * 6 = 1320 total damage!

Example 2: Your are seeing how much your Pokémon collection is worth. You have 13 cards that are each worth $18. How much are those cards worth in total?

13 * 18 = 234 dollars!
