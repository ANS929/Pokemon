About the Pokémon TCG

Pokémon was invented by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori. The first two Pokémon games, Red and Green, were released in Japan on 27 February 1996. The games were released in the US in 1998 as Red and Blue. The Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) was created after the success of the video games. The first set was released on 20 October 1996 in Japan, and the cards were introduced to North America in 1999. Pokémon tournaments began, and the first World Championships (Worlds) was held in 2000. Worlds has been held annually since 2004, except in 2020 and 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
